Quick answers

No. LineUp is a skill-based trading platform. Your results depend on reading the game and the market, managing entries, exits, position size and risk. It is intended for users 18 and over, and trading carries the risk of financial loss.
Each player trades on a live order book. A fair-value model nudges the reference price on every on-field event, runs, wickets, milestones, and the book re-clears as buyers and sellers react. The price you see is the last traded price between real orders, not a bookmaker's odds.
Every position has a clock. Scalp settles within an over, Day settles at the end of a match, Swing runs across a Test or series, and Position holds through a whole tournament. You pick the horizon when you place the trade.
Add and withdraw instantly over UPI from your in-app wallet. Balances are held in a segregated escrow account, and every transaction is logged in your history. You can start trading with as little as ₹10.
Trader funds are kept separate from company funds in escrow, KYC keeps the market clean, and per-player circuit limits guard against runaway prices. Every order and fill is timestamped and auditable.
LineUp lists 180+ players across international and major league cricket. Markets open ahead of each fixture and trade live ball-by-ball during the game, with deeper liquidity while a match is in play.

Risk warning. Trading on LineUp involves financial risk and may not be suitable for everyone. Prices can move against you. This is a skill-based product for users 18 and over. Please trade responsibly and only with money you can afford to lose.
