How it works

Four ways to trade the game

Every position has a horizon. Scalp a single over, ride a match, or hold a player for the whole tournament, each settles on its own clock.

Scalp Auto · live

Seconds to minutes. Trade ball-by-ball as a bowler runs in, prices tick on every delivery.

Day Settles today

One match. Open at the toss, settle at the last ball. Your call on who has the day.

Swing Multi-day

Days to weeks. Build a position across a Test or a series and ride the form curve.

Position Long-term

The match moves the market

A player's price isn't a number we make up. It reacts, ball by ball, to what's actually happening on the field, and to where the order book sits.

  • See the move on the chart. Boundary and wicket markers sit right on the line so you can read the story.
  • No manual marking. Prices clear against live buy & sell orders, not a bookmaker's spread.
